## Die kurze Antwort

<!-- ÜBERSETZEN: A Bitcoin wallet is software (or hardware) that stores your private keys - the cryptographic passwords that prove you own specific bitcoin. The bitcoin itself never leaves the blockchain; your wallet just holds the keys to access it.

Think of it like this: Bitcoin is like a safety deposit box that exists in public view. Everyone can see what's inside, but only you have the key to open it. Your wallet holds that key. -->
